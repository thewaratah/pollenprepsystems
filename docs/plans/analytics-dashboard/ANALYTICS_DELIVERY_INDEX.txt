PROJECT OVERVIEW
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Build a Google Sheet analytics dashboard that automatically fetches data from
Airtable and displays 7 key business intelligence metrics for kitchen prep
operations:

  1. Prep Efficiency (daily) — % of ordered items matching prep output
  2. Waste Indicators (daily) — Items above/below par levels
  3. Recipe Popularity (weekly) — Top 10 recipes by frequency
  4. Staff Workload (weekly) — Ordering distribution (Gooch/Sabs or Evan)
  5. Feedback Trends (real-time) — Resolution rates, open issues
  6. Automation Health (real-time) — Script success rates, execution times
  7. Cross-Venue Comparison (daily/weekly) — Sakura vs Waratah side-by-side

Timeline: 5 weeks to full deployment (40 hours/week developer effort)
